Biography

Hi, I'm Yifan Zhong. I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student in the PAIR Lab at Peking University guided by Prof. Yaodong Yang. Prior to this, I earned my bachelor's degree from Tongji University in Computer Science and Technology. Earlier in my education, I attended the first experimental class at The High School Affiliated to Renmin University of China.

My research interests include reinforcement learning and large language model. Please do not hesitate to reach out to me via email at zhongyifan@stu.pku.edu.cn !
